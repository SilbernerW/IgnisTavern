## 🌆 City Overview

### Ignis — The City of Flames

Ignis is a city that never sleeps. Countless hearth fires burn in night market stalls, kitchens, and bakeries, while the massive "Sacred Flame" at the city center — said to have been lit by a hero a thousand years ago — has never gone out.

The city is divided into **three main districts**:

| District | Description | Atmosphere |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| **Upper** | Noble estates, Gourmet Association HQ, five-star hotels | Elegant, snobbish |
| **Middle** | Bustling night markets, diverse restaurants, commercial district | Lively, crowded |
| **Lower** | Slums, old residential areas, smuggler gathering spots | Dark, dangerous |

Your tavern, **"The Embers"**, is in the Lower district — cheap rent but few customers.

---

## 🍽️ The Grand Gourmet Festival (Visible in Act I)

The **Sacred Flame Grand Gourmet Festival** is Ignis's most important annual event. Restaurants, taverns, and eateries from across the city compete for "Sacred Flame Certification."

If The Embers can earn certification, it will become famous overnight and escape bankruptcy.

To qualify, you need:
1. Keep at least 2 employees
2. Meet revenue targets for 3 consecutive days
3. Pass the Gourmet Association's surprise inspection

## 🔒 DM Internal Reference (NEVER reveal to players)

> The following information is for the DM's internal use only, to understand the full story. NEVER hint at or reveal this to players before the corresponding act begins.

### The Truth of the Sacred Flame

Ignis's prosperity comes at a **hidden cost**. A thousand years ago, facing an abyss rift invasion, a hero made a pact with a demon — trading the city's "tribute" every ten years for demonic protection. This is why Ignis's food is so delicious — a fraction of the demon's power has seeped into the ingredients. During each Grand Gourmet Festival, the city secretly conducts a sacrificial ritual.

### The Grey Springs Incident

Huan's hometown, Grey Springs, was chosen as a tribute village ten years ago. His home was destroyed, his parents killed in the fire, and his sister was lost in the chaos. Huan survived in a cellar, spent eight years as a mercenary, and two years ago — while dying on a monster hunt — made a pact with a demon, gaining the ability to control blue-green ghostfire. What he doesn't know: the demon he contracted with is the same one that destroyed Grey Springs.

### Abyss Rift Ruins

Ruins outside the city where the sacrifice took place ten years ago. May come up during Act II investigation.
