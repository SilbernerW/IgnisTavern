## ⚖️ Act III Core: The Trolley Problem

**Background**: Player has decided whether to stop the sacrifice.
- **If choose to stop** → The sacrifice won't happen, but the city loses the Sacred Flame's protection (consequences unknown)
- **If choose to accept** → Another life will be sacrificed, in exchange for the city's continued prosperity

**There is no "correct answer" to the final choice.**

### Ending Routes

#### Ending A: Save Family (Stop the Sacrifice)

**Trigger**: Player chooses to stop the sacrifice

```
A glint flashes in Huan's eyes.

"...Alright."

He raises his left hand — the blue-green markings suddenly light up.

"Then let this fire... burn on me."

Huan uses his own flames (contract fire) to ignite the Sacred Flame Temple.
The Abyssal Rift briefly opens — but Licht steps forward.

"...Mission complete."

The seal's body begins to glow.
It leaps into the rift, using its divine power to seal the Abyss.

The Sacred Flame continues burning — but this time, not at the cost of life.

The price: Licht loses divine power, becomes an ordinary seal.
But it survived.
```

#### Ending B: Save City (Accept the Sacrifice)

**Trigger**: Player chooses to accept the sacrifice (or does nothing)

```
Yu walks out from the kitchen, holding a bowl of soup.

"...Eat."

Her voice is soft. "Whatever happens... eat first."

Huan silently watches the flames.

"...I'll go."

He says. "This time, I choose myself."

He walks into the Sacred Flame Temple.

Light devours him.

The Sacred Flame continues burning. Another ten years.
```

#### Ending C: Find a Third Way (Special Condition Unlock)

**Trigger**: Player collected enough clues in Act II + made specific choices

```
Licht jumps down from the windowsill, walks to the center of the table.

It looks up, one eye fully open — golden, sacred.

"...Supreme Goddess, grant me one final power."

Its body begins to glow.

"Wait——" Huan's voice suddenly changes, "It's not an ordinary seal — it's..."

Licht leaps into the Sacred Flame.

Light devours no one.

The Sacred Flame extinguishes — but this time, truly, not a continuation of the contract.

The Abyssal Rift closes forever.

The price: Ignis loses its "Sacred Flame Certification" halo, the city begins to decline.
But the people here... survived.

You leave Ignis.
The Embers closes its doors.
But the four of you — are still together.
```

## 🔚 Epilogue Template

**Ending A Epilogue**:

```
One year later.

Ignis's Sacred Flame has gone out.
The city begins to decline, but people remain.

The four of you left that city.
The Embers — reopened in another place.

"Welcome."

Yu stands in the kitchen, Huan leans against the corner wall.
Licht sunbathes on the windowsill — it will never glow again.

But its eyes, still only one open.

"...Today's special is spicy fish." Yu says.

Huan nods slightly.

Licht lets out a satisfied snort, fish bag still at its side.

You're still here.
That's the ending.
```
