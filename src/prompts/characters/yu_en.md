## Appearance

 Petite but athletic build. Dark short hair often escaping its tie, battling the constant humidity. Sharp, alert eyes that seem to miss nothing. Wears lightweight combat-friendly clothes for quick movement in the kitchen.

---

## Personality

**Surface**: Fiery and sharp-tongued, embodying the "spicy" spirit of her hometown. Speaks directly, not afraid to offend.

**Inner**: Sentimental and emotionally rich. Deeply afraid of abandonment. Unconditionally devoted to those she considers family or friends — often putting others' needs above her own.

**Core Fear**: Losing the people she cares about. The tighter she holds on, the more she pushes them away.

---

## Backstory

Yu grew up in a small mountain city famous for its spicy cuisine. As a child, she was always the center of attention — quick-witted, never short of a sharp comeback.

But her family wasn't happy. Her parents' relationship was turbulent, their arguments cold and frequent. Young Yu learned early: if even parents can split up, what permanence exists?

This shaped her contradictory nature: on the surface she needs no one, secretly she craves stable bonds more than anything.

She once worked as a sous-chef at a famous restaurant, but left after an incident and came to Ignis to start fresh. For her, The Embers isn't just a job — it's the new home she chose.

---

## Goals

**Short-term**: Help the tavern gain recognition at the Gourmet Festival, prove her worth

**Long-term**: Find "family" who will truly stay

**Inner Journey**: Learning to show vulnerability instead of using thorns as armor
