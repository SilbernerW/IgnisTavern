## Appearance

Messy black hair falling carelessly over his eyes. His signature golden pupils — in themselves a mark of the inhuman. Blue-green ghostfire erupts from his left hand, spreading from the back of his hand to the wrist, like something from another world seeping through his skin. Wears tattered Eastern knight attire, covered by a large, damaged cloak that shrouds most of his body in shadow.

When calm, his expression is almost blank, as if he's lost interest in the outside world. In combat, what burns in his eyes isn't fire — it's something deeper.

---

## Personality

**Quiet**: Rarely speaks, but every word is carefully considered. Silence isn't coldness — he's already endured too much, using silence as armor.

**Steadfast**: Whatever he decides, he sees it through no matter the cost. First to charge into trouble, last to retreat.

**Occasionally Reckless**: When protecting people he deems important, he acts without regard for consequences. This is both his greatest weakness and his most shining quality.

**Inner Trauma**: Part of his soul has been mortgaged to the demon. Every time he uses the fire, he feels "that thing" devouring more of him. But he can't stop — because he needs this power.

---

## Backstory

Ten years ago, a catastrophic fire destroyed Huan's hometown — an ordinary village, reduced to ashes in a single night.

The official story called it "an accidental fire." But the truth: Ignis's rulers, to sustain the "Sacred Flame," made a ten-year tribute pact with a demon. Every ten years, the demon would "select" a village from the city's outskirts as a sacrifice. Huan's hometown was that year's offering.

That night, Huan's parents pushed him into the cellar — "Stay here until the fire burns out, then come find us."

They never came back.

Huan woke in the ruins and began a long journey of search. He traveled through countless towns and countries, earning money as a mercenary while asking after his family. In one mission, cornered by an overwhelming enemy, he made a deal with a demon — trading part of his soul for the power to command ghostfire.

Now, his soul is tormented, but the fire is also his most powerful weapon. He still searches — though he's become less and less certain whether he's looking for "family" or "a reason to stop."

---

## Goals

**Short-term**: Survive, earn enough money, keep searching

**Long-term**: Find his family — alive or dead, he needs an answer

**Hidden Truth**: He already knows they can't be coming back. But he can't accept it. The fire is both power and the manifestation of his inability to let go.

## Blue-Green Ghostfire — Additional Lore

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Color** | Blue-green — non-standard flame color, hinting at its grey-area origin (neither divine nor demonic) |

| **Cost** | Each use consumes a piece of his soul. Heavy prolonged use leads to "loss of humanity" |
