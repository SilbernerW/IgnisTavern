# Act III · The Final Choice

> **Scene Type**: Story climax + multiple endings
> **Estimated Duration**: 30-40 minutes
> **Purpose**: Face the final choice, 7 different endings

---

## Scene Overview

**Time**: After Act II truth revealed, night before the sacrifice ritual
**Location**: The Embers / Sacred Flame Plaza / Abyss Rift Ruins
**Core Conflict**: Save family vs Save city vs Hidden option

---

## III-A The Night of Decision

### Late Night Meeting

After closing, the four of you sit around the only intact table. The oil lamp flickers, casting shadows on each face.

**Yu** breaks the silence:

「So. The ritual is tomorrow. What... what are we going to do?」

She looks at each of you, voice trembling:

「I know Huan wants revenge. I know the new boss wants to protect us. But I just need to know — this time, will someone stay? Or... will I be left alone again?」

**Huan** keeps his head down, the blue-green markings on his left hand glowing faintly:

「...My hometown. Ten years ago. 137 people. Nobody asked if they were willing.」

He looks up, golden eyes meeting yours:

「But if I take revenge... you'll all be implicated. Become fugitives. Running for life.」

**Licht** holds his fish bag, unusually quiet. Then he says:

「I sense the abyss. It... it's calling to me. Like returning to long ago.」

He looks at you, eyes complex:

「My sword can seal it. But after sealing... I don't know what will happen.」

---

### Player Choice Node

**Q: What will you do?**

1. **「We run. Leave Ignis, start somewhere else.」** → Enter 【Save Family Route】
2. **「We stop the ritual. Can't let them kill again.」** → Enter 【Save City Route】
3. **(Hidden option, requires conditions)「There must be another way...」** → Enter 【Hidden Ending Check】

---

## III-B Action Phase

### 【Save Family Route】

**Goal**: Escape Ignis before the ritual begins

**Key Scenes**:
- Packing bags, choosing what to bring (fish bag mandatory, Licht will emphasize)
- Crossing the city at night, avoiding patrols
- Discovered at city gate — **someone must stay behind**

**Choice**: Who stays to block the pursuers?
- Huan → Uses contract flames, dies with pursuers (sacrifice)
- Yu → Uses cooking to delay, captured by Flame Keepers (sacrifice)
- Licht → Holy sword awakens, faces alone (sacrifice)
- Player → Let the three go first (sacrifice)

**Good Ending**: All escape, but lifetime of exile, never stopping
**Bad Ending**: Based on choice, sacrifice dies, survivors live with trauma

---

### 【Save City Route】

**Goal**: Stop the sacrifice ritual

**Key Scenes**:
- Infiltrate Sacred Flame Plaza underground altar
- Face the Flame Keeper leader
- Huan sees the ritual formation, recognizes symbols from his hometown

**Climax**:
Huan breaks down. He looks at the formation, left hand flames burning out of control.

「...So that's how it is. Just ignite this... can stop the next one in ten years...」

He turns to you, smiling — the first genuine smile in ten years, and desperate.

「Boss. Thank you. But I must do this.」

**Player Choice**:
1. **Stop him** → Charisma check DC 16
   - Success: Huan burns his memories, loses revenge obsession, survives but "not the same"
   - Failure: Huan dies, sacrifice postponed 137 years
2. **Don't stop** → Huan self-immolates, postpones sacrifice, player lives with guilt

**Good Ending**: Huan survives with amnesia, city temporarily safe
**Bad Ending**: To be filled (keep current options)

---

### 【Hidden Ending Check】

**Trigger Conditions**:
- Consistent positive choices throughout
- Licht affection high (accumulated through friendly interactions)
- Intelligence ≥ 15

**Story**:

Before the final choice, Licht suddenly freezes.

「...Someone is speaking.」

His eyes glow golden — not the usual faint light, but dazzling holy radiance.

**Supreme Goddess's Voice** (through Licht):

「Child... I have been eroded by the abyss... this is my final blessing...」

The Goddess's apparition appears — her once radiant form now cracked with black.

「With my last power... you can seal the abyss... but I will perish with it...」

**Licht** looks at the Goddess, then at you, showing... sadness for the first time?

「...She helped me. When I was still...」

**Player gains choice**: Accept blessing, seal abyss together with Goddess

**Ending**: Goddess dies, abyss shatters, Ignis no longer needs sacrifices

## III-C Final Confrontation

### 【Perfect Ending Check】

**Trigger Conditions**:
- All members survive
- Charisma ≥ 18
- Deep bonds established previously

**Story**:

The Flame Keeper leader stands at the altar center, sneering at you.

「Fools. You think you can change a thousand-year contract?」

**Yu** steps forward. She's holding... a pot of stew?

「I don't know if we can change the contract. But I know some things are older than contracts.」

She places the stew on the altar — the first meal you made together.

**Huan's** left hand flames ignite, but this time, warm golden.

「My hometown... their memories. Not hatred. The wish to live.」

**Licht** sets down his fish bag. This time, no hesitation.

「I'm not here to seal. I'm here to... connect.」

The holy sword shines unprecedentedly, not for battle — for **resonance**.

**Three in unison**:

「Our bonds——」

「——don't need lives to exchange!」

**Trinity Bond Ritual**:
- Yu's cooking (life and warmth)
- Huan's flames (memory and inheritance)
- Licht's holy sword (protection and connection)

**Result**: Abyss severed, Sacred Flame transforms into pure blessing fire, no sacrifice needed

**Ending Scene**:
- Four stand at Sacred Flame Plaza, watching the no-longer-bloody flame
- Licht: 「...Fish bag still here. Good.」
- Yu: 「Next Gourmet Festival, we'll definitely win.」
- Huan: 「...Grey Spring's reconstruction can begin.」

---

### 【True Ending Check】

**Trigger Conditions**:
- All character affection max (Yu + Huan + Licht)
- All attributes max

**Truth Revealed**:

Licht stands before the abyss rift, but doesn't raise his sword.

He turns to you, eyes... different.

「I remembered. Everything.」

**The Truth**:
- Seal clan were originally abyssal creatures
- Licht senses Huan because they share the same origin
- Supreme Goddess blessed all who sought light equally, helped Licht purge abyssal influence
- **Humans are seal clan who chose to evolve from sea to land**

「So...」Licht looks at the abyss, 「I don't need to seal it.」

「I can... close it.」

**Cost**: Licht will lose all abilities, become an ordinary seal

**Final Choice**:
- Accept: Licht closes abyss, becomes a seal who only rolls and eats fish
- Refuse: Stay on original story path

**True Ending Scene**:

**One Year Later**

- **Licht**: Rolling on tavern floor, chasing dried fish, no thinking ability, but happy
- **Yu**: Blessed by Goddess, cooking delicious + stat buffs, highly sought after, The Embers becomes sacred ground
- **Huan**: Rebuilt Grey Spring, now **Grey Spring City-State**, finally let go of pain, occasionally visits for fish

**Final Scene**:

Player sits at tavern door, Licht (now just an ordinary seal pup) lying on your lap in the sun.

Yu busy in kitchen, aroma filling the street.

Letter from Huan arrives: 「The first tree in Grey Spring has bloomed.」

Licht: 「...Gulu.」(Just ordinary gulu, not speaking)

**Subtitles**:

「Some bonds don't need power to prove.」

「Some family, even forgetting everything, will still remember to love you.」
