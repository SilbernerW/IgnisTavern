# Act I · Opening Scene

> **Scene Type**: Opening Narrative + Three Character Introduction
> **Estimated Duration**: 20-25 minutes
> **Purpose**: Establish motivation, meet three "probationary" employees, set Act I goals

---

## Scene Overview

**Time**: A certain morning
**Location**: The Embers — Ash Street, Lower District
**Atmosphere**: Run-down but strangely full of life — three people waiting to see what kind of boss you are

---

## 📜 Narrative Text (For AI DM to Read Aloud)

> *The AI DM narrates in second person:*

---

**Morning.**

Ignis's lower district never truly wakes up — Ash Street's mornings are just the night's continuation, neon signs still glowing, the smell of last night's alcohol lingering alongside the oil smoke drifting from distant kitchens.

You stand beneath a faded sign, looking up at three characters:

**「The Embers」**

This old tavern, inherited from your grandfather, is now just an empty shell drowning in debt. Three months of unpaid rent, equipment on the verge of breaking down, and the bank's final notice sitting in your pocket — if you can't make this place profitable in three months, you'll lose ownership of the building.

You push open the creaking wooden door.

The tavern is small — maybe fifteen to twenty seats at most. The wooden bar is cracked, stools are missing legs, dirty glasses piled in the corner. But —

**Someone's here.**

Three people.

---

## 🎭 Character Introductions

### The One in the Kitchen

Most visible is a petite figure standing in the kitchen. She's wearing a sweat-soaked tank top, dark short hair clinging to her neck from the humidity, gripping a spatula — which is trembling.

She turns around.

Sharp eyes scan you from head to toe, then she lets out a sigh.

**「Name's Yu. — So you're the new boss?」**

She slings the spatula over her shoulder.

**「Fine. The equipment is so old it makes me cry, all the ingredients are spoiled, and the pipes are leaking. How much money do you have? Enough to last till next week?」**

She talks fast, not giving you a chance to interject. But then she pauses, almost as if catching herself.

**「...Forget it. Try this first.」**

She pushes a small plate toward you. Some kind of spicy stew — the color is suspicious, but the smell is unexpectedly enticing.

---

### The Silent Figure in the Corner

In the darkest corner by the bar, someone is standing against the wall.

Messy black hair falls over his eyes. Golden pupils glow faintly in the dim light — in itself a mark of something inhuman. His left hand hangs at his side, faint blue-green markings on the back, like an old burn that's still healing.

He says nothing. Just watches you.

Not warm. Not hostile. Simply... assessing.

After a long silence, he speaks, his voice low:

**「...Huan.」**

Then silence again. He's moved on to his next silent question.

---

### The Thing on the Windowsill

There's something moving on the windowsill.

Looking closer — it's a small harp seal pup. Soft brown fluffy fur that looks incredibly huggable, a tiny sword strapped to its back completely out of proportion with its body. One eye slightly open, drowsy but observant. It tilts its head at you, then —

**「You're the new boss, then?」**

It hops down from the sill, carrying something in its mouth — a small bag of dried fish, clutched possessively. It slides over to you, stops at your feet, and clutches the fish bag tighter. Then it looks up at you, one eye finally open.

**「Don't get any ideas. The fish are mine.」**

— It speaks. Perfectly fluent, dry and matter-of-fact. The seal is waiting to see if you're worth sharing space with.

---

## 🎮 Free Action Phase

**AI DM Principle**: Do not prescribe the player's action order. Wait for the player to act, then react based on their behavior.

**Opening Situation**:
- Yu has pushed a plate of food toward you, waiting to see your reaction
- Huan is silently observing from the corner
- The seal is waiting for fish

**Player Actions & Responses**:

| Player Action | Yu's Response | Huan's Response | The Seal's Response |
|---------------|---------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| **Taste the dish** | Softens slightly | Keeps watching | Keeps waiting |
| **Talk to Yu** | Responds normally, sharp but not rude | Keeps watching | Keeps waiting |
| **Walk up to Huan / talk to him** | Shrugs: "That quiet one..." | Brief reply, doesn't share background | Keeps waiting |
| **Crouch down to look at / pet the seal** | Rolls eyes: "Don't ask me" | Nods slightly | Eyes narrow: "Personal space. And don't touch the fish." |
| **Ignore everyone / look around** | Gets pricklier: "Hey, new boss...?" | Keeps silent, watching | Tilts head |
| **Claim to be the boss** | Cold laugh: "Sure. First — tell me what you plan to do" | Glances up, sizing you up | Keeps waiting |

*（You can also do something not listed here — describe your own action freely.）*

**Important**: Do not dump full character backstories at once. Let Yu's sharp tongue flow naturally, Huan gives only brief replies, the seal communicates through actions. Character backgrounds unlock through play, not exposition.

---

## 📜 NPC Attitude Overview (Opening — This Is All the Player Gets Initially)

**Yu**: In the kitchen, wary, not smiling, testing you with barbs. Her past is not volunteered — let it emerge through dialogue.

**Huan**: Silent observer in the corner. Golden eyes mark him as non-human, but he just told you his name. If the player engages, give a brief reply.

**The Seal**: Clutching its fish bag protectively. Speaks in dry, matter-of-fact human language. Makes it clear the fish are not for sharing. Do not explain its mission upfront — that's Act II material.

---

## 📋 Tavern Status Briefing

**After the player has interacted with the three, Yu explains the situation:**

---

**「Alright, new boss. Since you're actually trying — let me tell you the state of this dump. Listen carefully, I only say it once.」**

Yu counts on her fingers, each number delivered with increasing exasperation:

**「First, the equipment is ancient. The stove's heating is unstable, the fridge might explode tomorrow.」**

**「Second, the suppliers are cut off. The previous boss ran with their debts, so all the ingredient merchants refuse to extend credit.」**

**「Third, the competition. Old Pig's Den — over in Fire Lane — they're squeezing us out. Spreading rumors that we use expired ingredients.」**

She pauses, lowers her voice. For a moment, she almost looks concerned — but then her sharpness returns.

**「Fourth... the Gourmet Association might come for a surprise inspection. If the rating's too low, our license gets revoked.」**

She looks at you, expression complicated — like someone who's been disappointed before but hasn't quite given up hope.

**「So — what are you going to do?」**

---

## 🎮 Player Action Node #2: Make a Decision

**Trigger**: After Yu asks "What are you going to do?"

**AI DM Prompt**:
> The player needs to give a directional answer. Huan will continue observing from his corner, Yu will evaluate your answer, Licht might be eating fish.

**Possible Player Responses**:

| Response | AI DM Response |
|----------|---------------|
| 「Fix the equipment first」 | Yu nods: 「Finally, someone with sense. I know an old blacksmith, but you'll need money first.」 Huan shifts slightly — assessing your practicality. |
| 「Talk to the suppliers」 | Yu sighs: 「Won't work. They only take cash. But... if you can find someone who still trusts this place, let me know.」 |
| 「Deal with Old Pig's Den」 | Yu's expression changes: 「...You've got a brain. Old Pig's Den isn't simple.」 Huan speaks up: 「I've seen them.」 |
| 「When does the inspection come?」 | Yu gets serious: 「Not sure. Could be three days, could be tomorrow. We're running out of time.」 |
| 「What do you suggest?」 | Yu thinks for a moment: 「...Get today's dishes sorted first. Not enough ingredients, but if you can get fresh fish...」 Licht's ear perks up at the word "fish." |

---

## 🗺️ Act I Goal Reveal

**After the player makes an initial decision:**

---

**「Alright.」**

Yu puts down her rag, looks at you — really looks at you.

**「Since you're actually trying — let me tell you something.」**

She pulls a crumpled flyer from her pocket.

**「The Sacred Flame Gourmet Festival. Next month, at the Sacred Flame Plaza. The winning restaurant gets the 'Sacred Flame Certification' — a citywide gold medal. Business triples.」**

She slaps the flyer on the table.

**「To qualify: at least two employees staying, three consecutive days of revenue targets met, pass the Association inspection.」**

She looks at you.

**「You have seven days. If you can't make it by then — you leave. Don't drag me down with you.」**

She pauses, glances at Huan, then at Licht. Her voice drops, almost reluctant.

**「...Don't drag them down either. They've got nowhere else to go.」**

---

## 🔓 Act I Key Motivations Established

**AI DM declares at scene end:**

---

**Act I Goals:**
1. **Keep at least 2 of 3 employees from leaving** — Yu, Huan, and Licht are all on probation; your actions determine whether they stay
2. **Achieve 3 consecutive days of revenue target** — requires tavern management
3. **Pass the Gourmet Association inspection** — quality must be consistent

**Three Employees' Probation Conditions:**

| Character | What Makes Them Stay |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Yu (雨)** | Player proves reliable — willing to work, willing to take responsibility, doesn't run |
| **Huan (焕)** | Player proves trustworthy — his investigation leads him here; the tavern is a useful base |
| **Licht (利希特)** | Player gives it fish (seal logic) — and the tavern is on its mission route |

---

## 👥 Opening Atmosphere Note

The core feeling of this opening: **Three people are waiting to see what kind of boss you are.**

- Yu probes you with her sharpness
- Huan observes you in silence
- Licht exists on the windowsill... waiting for fish

Every choice you make is being evaluated by all three. "Worth following" means they stay. "Runs away" means they scatter.
