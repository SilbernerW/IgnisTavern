## 📜 Main Story Narrative Structure

### Act II Opening — Huan's Confession

**AI DM narration after trigger**:

Huan stands up from his corner and walks to you. This is the first time he's actively approached you.

```
Huan's golden pupils glow faintly in the dim light. He raises his left hand — blue-green patterns on the back of his hand are faintly visible in the darkness, like some kind of spreading mark.

"...I found something."

His voice is low. "About the Sacred Flame. About ten years ago."

He is silent for a moment.

"...I must tell you something. About who I am. About this city."
```

**After player responds**, Huan begins to tell his story:

```
"Ten years ago. The Sacred Flame Temple."

Huan's golden eyes stare at some distant place.

"There was a great fire. A 'sacrifice.'"

"Ignis offers one life to the Abyssal Rift every ten years — in exchange for the Sacred Flame's burning, in exchange for the city's prosperity."

"My... my hometown was the one chosen ten years ago."
```

## 🕯️ The Sacred Flame Truth (Background)

**AI DM reveals through narration**:

> The Sacred Flame of Ignis is not a miracle, it's a **bargain**.
> Ten years ago, the city made a pact with the Abyssal Rift — offer one life every ten years, and the Sacred Flame continues to burn.
> Ten years ago, what was sacrificed was Huan's hometown — a small village called "Gray Springs Town."
> That "great fire" killed 137 people. No one asked why.
> The Sacred Flame continued to burn. The city continued to prosper.
> And the one who enacted the contract was an organization called the "Flame Keepers" — now, behind the Gourmet Association.
