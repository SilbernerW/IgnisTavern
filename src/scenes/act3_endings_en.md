# Act III · Endings — Seven Finales

> **Scene Type**: Multiple endings presentation
> **Estimated Duration**: 5-10 minutes per ending
> **Purpose**: Present different endings based on player choices and attributes

---

## Ending 1: Save Family — Good Ending

**Trigger**: Choose to run, no one guards rear (all pass Dexterity check)

**Ending Scene**:

Three months later, border town.

A new tavern opens, sign reads 「Embers·Reborn」.

**Yu** busy in kitchen, shouting orders with her usual fire: 「Huan! Table three needs wiping! Licht, stop sunbathing and help!」 **Huan** wiping tables, **Licht** sunbathing on windowsill.

**Yu**: 「New boss, today's fish arrived.」 She pauses, then adds gruffly: 「...It's actually fresh this time. Don't mess it up.」

**Huan**: 「...It's quiet here. No Sacred Flame, no Flame Keepers.」

**Licht**: 「Fish is fresh. But still keeping tally.」

**Player**: 「Will we keep running forever?」

**Huan**: 「...At least, we're together.」

**Subtitles**: 「Lifetime exile, never stopping. But with family by your side, anywhere is home.」

---

## Ending 2: Save Family — Bad Ending

**Trigger**: Choose to run, someone guards rear and sacrifices

**Different versions based on choice**:

### Huan Sacrifices

**Huan**: 「Go! My flames... can hold them!」

Contract flames erupt, gold and blue-green intertwine, consuming pursuers and Huan.

**Yu**: 「...Huan!」

**Licht**: 「...He chose. We respect.」

**Aftermath**: Survivors live with guilt, Yu and Licht grow closer, but forever an empty space.

### Yu Sacrifices

**Yu**: 「I... I'll delay. You go first.」

Captured by Flame Keepers, becomes early sacrifice.

**Huan**: 「...I should have stayed. Should have been me.」

**Aftermath**: Huan falls into deep guilt, Licht becomes the glue, but team incomplete.

### Licht Sacrifices

**Licht**: 「...Take care of the fish bag. I'll come back for it.」

Holy sword awakens, brilliant light. Faces pursuers alone, never returns.

**Yu**: 「...It said it would keep tally.」

**Aftermath**: Yu and Huan escape with Licht's fish bag, occasionally speak to it.

### Player Sacrifices

**Player**: 「You go first. I... will hold them.」

**Huan**: 「...Why?」

**Yu**: 「No! We can go together——」

**Aftermath**: Three escape, but team falls apart. Huan takes revenge alone, Yu returns to old life, Licht continues wandering.

---

## Ending 3: Save City — Good Ending

**Trigger**: Stop Huan successfully (Charisma check passed)

**Ending Scene**:

Ritual destroyed, Flame Keepers scattered.

**Huan** lies on ground, left hand flames extinguished, eyes empty.

**Huan**: 「...Who am I? Why... am I here?」

He lost all memories — revenge, hometown, even you.

**Yu**: 「...He's alive. That's enough.」 She crosses her arms, looking away. 「...Don't look at me like that. I'm just stating facts. It's not like I was worried or anything.」

**Licht**: 「...Memories can rebuild. People can't.」

**One Year Later**:

Huan becomes tavern helper, hardworking but silent. Occasionally looks at his left hand, wondering what it is.

**Player**: 「Huan, today's fish is here.」

**Huan**: 「...Thank you. We... have we known each other long?」

**Subtitles**: 「He's not who he was. But he's alive. Is this a good ending? You decide.」

---

## Ending 4: Save City — Bad Ending

**Trigger**: Fail to stop Huan, or don't stop

**Ending Scene**:

**Huan** jumps into formation, contract flames erupt from within.

**Huan**: 「...Everyone at Grey Spring... I...」

Flames consume him, consume the formation.

Ritual interrupted, but Huan also disappears.

**Yu**: 「...He did it. He took revenge.」 Her voice is hollow. Then, sharper: 「...Idiot. We could have found another way. There always is one.」

**Licht**: 「...The cost is. He became the flame itself.」

**Aftermath**:

Sacrifice postponed 137 years. Ignis continues prosperous, but everyone knows, in 137 years new sacrifice needed.

Yu and Licht stay at tavern, but never speak Huan's name again.

**Subtitles**: 「Revenge succeeded. But the avenger is gone. Is this victory?」

---

## Ending 5: Hidden Ending

**Trigger**: Positive choices + Licht affection high + INT ≥ 15

**Ending Scene**:

**Supreme Goddess's** apparition appears before abyss rift, broken and cracked with black.

**Goddess**: 「With my last power... seal it...」

**Licht**: 「...Thank you. For once helping me.」

Holy sword resonates with Goddess's power, light consumes the abyss.

Goddess's apparition shatters, dissipates with the abyss.

**Aftermath**:

Ignis's Sacred Flame becomes ordinary fire, no longer needs sacrifice.

But Goddess is forever gone.

**Licht**: 「...She's gone. But mission complete.」

**Subtitles**: 「God falls, mortals free. Is this the best ending? Or just another beginning?」

---

## Ending 6: Perfect Ending

**Trigger**: All survive + Charisma ≥ 18 + deep bonds

**Final Check Battle**:

**Round 1**: Will DC 16 (resist abyss erosion)
- **Success**: Stay conscious, continue ritual
- **Failure**: Someone temporarily失控, needs rescue

**Round 2**: Coordination DC 18 (three-person sync)
- **Success**: Trinity resonance successful
- **Failure**: Need extra round to adjust

**Round 3**: Final burst DC 20 (or any attribute max)
- **Success**: Perfect ending achieved
- **Failure**: Convert to normal good ending

**Ending Scene**:

Flame Keeper leader: 「Fools! You think you can change a thousand-year contract?」

**Yu** takes out a pot of stew, her hands steady despite everything: 「I don't know if we can change the contract. But I know some things are older than contracts.」 She glances at the others, almost defensive. 「...And yes, I made this specially. Don't ask me why. I'm just hungry.」

**Huan**: 「My hometown... their memories. Not hatred. The wish to live.」

**Licht** sets down fish bag: 「I'm not here to seal. I'm here to connect.」

**Three in unison**: 「Our bonds — don't need lives to exchange!」

**Trinity Bond Ritual**:

- Yu's cooking (life and warmth)
- Huan's flames (memory and inheritance)
- Licht's holy sword (protection and connection)

Light consumes the abyss, not seal, but **severance**.

Sacred Flame transforms into pure blessing fire, no sacrifice needed.

**Ending Scene**:

Four stand at Sacred Flame Plaza, watching no-longer-bloody flame.

**Licht**: 「...Fish bag still here. Good.」

**Yu**: 「Next Gourmet Festival, we'll definitely win.」

**Huan**: 「...Grey Spring's reconstruction can begin.」

**Subtitles**: 「Bonds defeated contract. Family defeated fate. Is this a fairy tale? No, this is the ending you earned.」

## Ending 7: True Ending

**Trigger**: All character affection max + all attributes max

**Truth Revealed**:

**Licht**: 「I remembered. Everything.」

- Seal clan were originally abyssal creatures
- Licht senses Huan because they share same origin
- Supreme Goddess blessed all who sought light equally
- **Humans are seal clan who chose to evolve from sea to land**

**Licht**: 「I don't need to seal it. I can... close it.」

**Cost**: Lose all abilities, become ordinary seal

**Player**: 「Are you sure?」

**Licht**: 「...Fish in the bag, remember to feed them.」

---

### One Year Later

**The Embers** has become sacred ground of Ignis.

**Licht**: Rolling on floor, chasing dried fish, no thinking ability, only "gulu gulu." But happy.

**Yu**: Blessed by Goddess, cooking delicious + stat buffs, highly sought after. Signature dish 「Bond Stew」.

**Huan**: Rebuilt Grey Spring, now **Grey Spring City-State**. Finally let go of pain, occasional letters: 「First tree has bloomed.」

**Final Scene**:

Player sits at tavern door, Licht (ordinary seal pup) lying on lap in sun.

Yu busy in kitchen, aroma filling street.

Huan's letter on table.

**Licht**: 「...Gulu.」(Just ordinary gulu, not speaking)

**Subtitles**:

「Some bonds don't need power to prove.」

「Some family, even forgetting everything, will still remember to love you.」

「True Ending·Complete」

## Ending 8: Despair Ending

**Trigger**: All character affection <20 (extremely poor relationships, team falls apart)

**Condition**: When player at final choice, unable to convince team or take effective action

---

### Beginning of Collapse

**Yu** looks at you, eyes changing from hope to disappointment:

「...You never cared about us, did you?」

「Three months, you never truly cared about anyone. Just... used us.」

**Huan's** flames extinguish, voice cold:

「...I thought you were different. But you're no different from Flame Keepers.」

**Licht** picks up fish bag, turns to leave:

「...Fish won't be shared. Family won't either.」

---

### Lonely Choice

Team falls apart.

**Yu** leaves tavern alone, disappears into night. Found as early sacrifice three days later.

**Huan** goes to Sacred Flame Plaza alone, tries to destroy ritual alone, surrounded by Flame Keepers, contract flames go wild, turns to ash with half the plaza.

**Licht** senses abyss calling, chooses to return — not as holy knight, but as abyss's servant.

**Player** stands alone in empty tavern, holding bank's final notice: tavern will be auctioned.

---

### Night of Ritual

Sacrifice proceeds on schedule.

But this time, ritual goes out of control — because of Huan's death, contract flames create chain reaction.

Abyss rift expands, not consuming one person, but consuming entire Ignis.

City burns in flames, not Sacred Flame's warmth, but abyss's hunger.

Player stands at city gate, watching everything destroyed.

---

### Final Scene

**One Year Later**

Ignis becomes ruins, called 「City of Ash」.

Occasional adventurers enter, see a lonely figure wandering in ruins —

Former tavern owner, gone mad, only repeats one sentence:

「...I should have chosen... I should have...」

**Subtitles**:

「Some mistakes cannot be undone.」

「Some loneliness is deserved.」

「Despair Ending·Complete」
