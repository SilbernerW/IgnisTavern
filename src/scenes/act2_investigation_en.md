# Act II · Investigation — Beneath the Sacred Flame

> **Scene Type**: Plot progression + Mystery exploration
> **Estimated Duration**: 15-20 minutes
> **Purpose**: Discover anomalies behind the city's prosperity, establish investigation clues, pave way for revelation

---

## Scene Overview

**Time**: One week after qualification confirmation, before the Culinary Competition
**Location**: Various locations in Ignis City (Sacred Flame Plaza, Archives, Underground Market)
**Atmosphere**: Surface prosperity masking underlying unease

---

## Opening: Culinary Competition Invitation

Morning. A gold-embossed invitation arrives at the Ashes Tavern.

**Yu** holds the letter, brows furrowed deeply:

"...Ignis Culinary Competition. Officially sponsored. The winner gets to attend the 'Blessing Ceremony' at Sacred Flame Plaza."

She slaps the letter on the table, lowering her voice: "But doesn't it seem strange? A small tavern like us, suddenly receiving an invitation?"

**Huan** looks at the fire-red seal on the invitation, his left-hand flame flickering slightly:

"This seal... I've seen something similar in Grey Spring. Before... the disaster."

**Licht** unusually makes no quip, the holy sword in his fish bag emitting faint resonance:

"...Ancient contract detected. Something is wrong."

---

## Investigation Branches

Players can choose investigation paths (multiple selections possible, affecting subsequent clues):

### Branch A: Archives Investigation

**Location**: Ignis City Archives

**Huan** suggests: "If this city has secrets, the archives will bear traces."

**Check**: Intelligence DC 12 (Search historical records)
- **Success**: Discover records from 137 years ago systematically "lost"—every 137 years there's a "city reconstruction"
- **Critical Success (≥18)**: Find a hand-drawn map marking "Old Grey Spring"—Huan's hometown
- **Failure**: Kindly "escorted" out by the archivist who says: "Some history is best left to professionals."

**Huan's Comment** (if present):

He stares at the hand-drawn map, fingers trembling slightly: "...Old Grey Spring. They didn't even keep the name. Just 'old.'"

He looks up, eyes hollow but burning with something: "...If this is the truth, then what was my suffering all these years? Residue of fuel?"

He carefully folds the map, voice low: "...I want to know everything. Not just Grey Spring, but before. All of it."

**Licht's Comment** (if present):

"...137 years," the holy sword in his fish bag hums uneasily, "my people remember this cycle. But we thought... it was legend."

He rarely skips the quips, voice carrying ancient weight: "...This isn't ordinary magic. It's a 'blood pact.' An ancient trade of souls for prosperity."

He looks out the window: "...The Supreme Goddess wouldn't allow this. If this pact truly exists, then either she doesn't know... or she's no longer with us."

**Yu's Comment**:

"...So every 137 years, this city 'reconstructs'? Sounds like some kind of..." She trails off, face pale. "...Just note it down. Don't jump to conclusions yet."

---

### Branch B: Underground Market Investigation

**Location**: Ignis Underground Black Market

**Licht** leads the way: "...Fish have their channels. Information too."

**Check**: Charisma DC 14 (Negotiate with black market merchants)
- **Success**: Merchant reveals "Sacred Flame" is not a blessing but "fuel"—the city's prosperity comes from "something burning"
- **Critical Success (≥18)**: Obtain a "Sacred Flame fragment"—touching it reveals faint wailing sounds
- **Failure**: Merchant suddenly clams up, saying "This topic isn't for surface discussion," and hurries away

**Licht's Comment** (as guide, he must be present):

He takes the fragment with his fin, the holy sword's light suddenly dimming: "...Hear that? Wailing. These souls... they're still inside."

His voice rarely serious, devoid of usual jest: "...This isn't ordinary fire. It's 'soul-prison flame.' Trapping the dead in eternal burning, never ceasing."

He returns the fragment, fish bag trembling slightly: "...My holy sword can sense suffering. These souls... have been burning for centuries. They want release, but the contract won't allow it."

**Huan's Comment** (if present):

The moment he touches the fragment, his left-hand flame dances wildly, face pale: "...This feeling. Just like Grey Spring's final day. The taste in the air... it's souls burning."

He steps back, voice breaking: "...My family... are they in here too? Burning in some fragment?"

He grabs your arm with surprising strength: "...We need to find the source. Not tomorrow. Now."

**Yu's Comment**:

She looks at the fragment, hands trembling slightly, but voice still sharp: "...Fuel? What's burning? Don't tell me—" She pauses, shoving the fragment back to you. "...Put it away. Don't look at it here."

---

### Branch C: Sacred Flame Plaza Observation

**Location**: Sacred Flame Plaza (Daytime)

**Huan** stands at the plaza's edge, left-hand flame dancing wildly:

"...It's calling me. No, calling what's inside me."

**Check**: Perception DC 13 (Observe the Sacred Flame)
- **Success**: Notice "faces" in the flame's light—fleeting expressions of pain
- **Critical Success (≥18)**: Hear whispers repeating "Save me" and "137 years"
- **Failure**: Only perceive the Sacred Flame as magnificent, the city's pride

**Huan's Comment** (as observer, he must be present):

His left-hand flame resonates with the Sacred Flame, face unusually pale in the red light: "...They're calling me. Those faces... they recognize what's inside me."

He closes his eyes, voice pained: "...The people of Grey Spring. I can feel them. They're still inside... still burning."

He suddenly opens his eyes, filled with determination: "...I'm going to save them. Whatever the contract says, whether 137 years or 1000. I'm going to let them rest."

**Licht's Comment** (if present):

"...Whispers," his voice rarely tense, "'Save me.' 137 years. This is no coincidence."

The holy sword in his fish bag shakes violently: "...This is a distress signal. Trapped souls using gaps in the contract... to send messages to the outside world."

He looks at Huan: "...You can hear them, right? Your flame shares their source. You are... their hope."

**Yu's Comment**:

She pulls you back a few steps, voice barely audible: "...Did you see them? The faces? Don't stare, you'll draw attention." She pauses, pretending to adjust her hair. "...We need to leave. Now."

---

## Clue Synthesis

Regardless of chosen paths, the team reunites in the evening to share findings:

**Huan** (if participated in Archives or Plaza investigation):

"...Grey Spring wasn't the first. Every 137 years, there's a 'Grey Spring' consumed by the 'Sacred Flame.'"

His voice hollow: "My family... just fuel. Fuel to keep this city prosperous."

**Yu's Reaction**:

She stands at the kitchen doorway, spatula in hand—but she's forgotten what she was doing.

"...So you mean," her voice soft but each word clear, "the money we earned this week, the competition we're preparing for, it's all... built on other people's corpses?"

She sets down the spatula, slowly walking over to stand beside Huan: "...Then we'll find out. Everything. The whole truth."

She looks at you, fear in her eyes but more anger: "...Don't tell me you want to give up. I finally felt at home here. If this home is built on lies, I want to know the whole truth."

**Licht** (if participated in Black Market investigation):

"...My people know of ancient contracts. But didn't know someone was continuing them."

He looks at his holy sword: "The Supreme Goddess wouldn't allow such trade. This is... blasphemy."

---

## Scene Conclusion: Late Night Resolution

After closing, the four sit around the table.

**Yu** breaks the silence:

"...So. The Culinary Competition is a trap. Or an opportunity."

She looks at each of you: "We could run. Leave now. But..."

She clenches her fists: "But what about the next Grey Spring? The next place consumed by the 'Sacred Flame'?"

She stands, voice regaining its characteristic sharpness, but this time with determination:

"...I'm done being lied to. Done pretending not to see. If this city is built on lies, I want to watch it crumble."

She looks at Huan: "...I'll help you find the truth. All of it. Then we'll decide together what to do."

**Scene ends, entering Act II · Revelation**
