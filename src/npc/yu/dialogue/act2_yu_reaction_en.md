## Event 1: Yu's Reaction Upon Learning Huan's Past

**Trigger**: Player chooses to tell Yu about Huan's truth

**Yu's Initial Reaction**:

```
"..."

Yu falls silent for a long time. The rag in her hand stops moving.

"So that guy... he..."

She keeps her head down, her expression hidden.

"...He stayed because of this city."
```

**Dialogue Tree**:

```
Option A: Comfort Huan
> "When the previous boss left, I waited for a month."
> "Huan has waited for ten years."

Option B: Anger
> "So this city—its prosperity was bought with other people's lives?"
> "The dishes I cook—were they made with their blood..."

Option C: Pragmatic
> "...So what do we do now?"
> "We have seven days."
```
